A collaborative experience

Have you ever been on a yacht? Probably not, you're broke.

But we have. And there is a common problem YachtRiders face - too many people that want to add songs to a single queued playlist. Luckily, we have a solution!

But...what is PartyPlaylist?

PartyPlaylist is a functional collaborative playlist that allows guests to any form of social gathering to add songs to a playlist. In addition to this they are given the opportunity to upvote songs they want to hear sooner to determine their position in the queue.
